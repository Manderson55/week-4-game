Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number when you click start playing

There are four crystals below. Each crystal will be assigned a random value from 1 to 12

When you click on a crystal you will add that value to your total score

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, if you go over, you loose!

Each time the game starts, the values for the crystals will change

Good luck!
